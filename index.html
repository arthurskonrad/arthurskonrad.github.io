ARTHUR SCHULLE KONRAD

About me

My name is Arthur but you can call me tuio, I was born in 1996, in Brazil. I am 26 years old and some of these are dedicated to programming and painting. I work as a front-end developer and I paint as a hobby.

One of the things I most appreciate when working today is the friendships acquired along the way.

Developer

I started working as a front-end in February 2017, it's been five years since I've been on this path.

In the beginning I focused a lot on learning javascript, css and html, in the purest way possible. Not to say that I didn't use any framework, I also studied a lot of jQuery.

I'm currently more focused on React, but I continue to value the understanding of Javascript Vanilla.

I work full time as a Front-end Developer at Leroy Merlin Brasil.

Painter

I started to be interested in painting in 2016, at the time I hadn't dedicated time and study to learn how to paint, it was only in 2020 that I started to venture into the arts.

My canvases are digitally painted, which makes the process a lot easier and I believe that the result are fine.

I usually make paintings of people, usually family and friends.
